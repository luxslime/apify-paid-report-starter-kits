Decisions this report supports

  • Detect new official recall records matching product watchlists
  • Group market-action evidence by portfolio or vehicle scope
  • Prepare a source-linked safety operations review queue

Data limitation: The report summarizes official public records and does not certify product safety or determine legal obligations.

Buyer-capped starter input

The maintained starter is expected to deliver one `safety-record-row` event at $0.006 and one `market-action-alert` event at $0.20 when the sample evidence still matches. The buyer-controlled cap remains $1.00.

  "watchlists": [

  ],
  "monitorKey": "starter-battery-recalls",
  "initialRunMode": "emit_backfill",
  "generateReport": false,
  "emitRawRows": true,
  "emitUnchanged": false,
  "emitExport": false,
  "maxChangedRecords": 3,
  "maxChargeUsd": 1,
  "dryRun": false
