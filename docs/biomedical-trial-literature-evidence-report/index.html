Decisions this report supports

  • Find registered trials with missing or unclear publication links
  • Compare trial status and literature evidence across a watchlist
  • Prepare a source-linked evidence review queue

Data limitation: Registry and citation metadata are research inputs, not clinical conclusions, causality findings, or medical advice.
